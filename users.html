Pedestrian Safety

Your Rights

  • Right-of-way at marked crosswalks
  • Safe sidewalk access in urban areas
  • Clear visibility at intersections

Your Responsibilities

  • Use crosswalks where available
  • Make eye contact with drivers
  • Avoid distractions (phones/headphones)
